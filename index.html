I'm Delante Desouza

Web Developer., Software Consultant.,Software Engineer., Dreamer.

I'm delante desouza and I'm a freelance web developer.

It all started when I was about six years old. I had this overwhelming feeling that I was going to do something extraordinary with technology. It was probably that same feeling that all nerdy 90's babies had as we grew up in the decade of the World Wide Web (back when 56kb dial-up was actually considered fast!)


But in my case, that feeling never quite went away.


From sneaking out of my room late at night to play Minesweeper on my Dad's Windows 98, to graduating from the University of Maryland with a degree in Computer Science, I can say that I've spent a good amount of time around technology, all the while thinking about the future of tech and the web.


In addition to being a freelance developer, I'm also a business management and software design consultant in a company created by myself and two friends of mine. At this point, I guess you could say that I am a world-changer that works with other world-changers to help make a difference through web development, consulting, and UX design.


So, that's me! Be sure to check out the other sections of my portfolio to see what I've done and hopefully you'll find that I can also help you!


Thanks for reading!
